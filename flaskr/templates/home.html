What's issa-mood?

You want to listen to a song that came out recently, but you're not sure if it'll fit the summer vibes and you're not trying to kill any vibes today.
Look no more, for issa-mood will check that for you!
We pull any song lyrics (or even speeches!) from Genius, and we'll compute the range of emotions the song might evoke.
Try it out below and type any song you're curious about!
